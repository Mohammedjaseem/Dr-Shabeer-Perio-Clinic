What is smile designing?

A Smile Design is the planning process for creating your individualized treatment plan to take your smile from what it is now into a smile you can be proud of and makes you feel more confident. Your Smile Design is essentially a treatment plan.

Which is better, implant or bridge?

A dental bridge will almost certainly have to be replaced whereas accurately placed implants can last 40 years or more. Implants have a significantly higher success rate compared to bridges. The cost of the dental bridge is initially less, but it may need to be replaced at some point in the future.
